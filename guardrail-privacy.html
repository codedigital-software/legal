Guardrail for Salesforce — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026 · Provided by Codedigital.

Guardrail is a browser extension that adds safety guardrails and a Setup command palette to Salesforce. This policy explains exactly what data it handles. In short: Guardrail stores your settings locally and uses a random device identifier to check your Pro licence. It does not collect your browsing history, your personal information, or any Salesforce business data.

What we store on your device

  • Settings — your guarded-action rules and detection preferences. Stored locally in the browser (chrome.storage) and synced across your own signed-in browser profile by the browser itself.
  • Licence cache — your current tier (Free/Pro) and its validity, cached locally so the extension works offline.
  • Device token — a random identifier generated on first run. It contains no personal information and is not derived from you, your machine, or your Salesforce account.

What we transmit, and to whom

  • Licence check — the extension sends your device token to our licensing backend (guardrail-api-9dkx.onrender.com) to return your current tier. The token is the only identifier sent; no email, name, or Salesforce data is included.
  • Payments — if you upgrade to Pro, checkout is handled entirely by Stripe. Your payment details are entered on Stripe’s hosted page and processed by Stripe; Guardrail never sees or stores card data. See Stripe’s privacy policy.

Your Salesforce data stays in your browser

Guardrail’s guardrails and command palette run entirely in the page. The extension does not read, collect, or transmit your Salesforce records, fields, or business data.

The optional high-accuracy detection setting (off by default) uses the cookies permission to read your Salesforce session cookie and query Organization.IsSandbox — a single call to your own Salesforce org to confirm whether it is a sandbox or production. That request goes directly from your browser to your org; the session cookie and the result are never sent to us or to any third party. If you leave high-accuracy detection off, the extension never accesses cookies at all.

What we do NOT collect

  • No browsing history, page content, or analytics/tracking.
  • No personal or contact information (other than what you provide to Stripe at payment).
  • No Salesforce records, business data, credentials, or session tokens.

Data retention

Local data stays on your device until you remove the extension or clear its storage. Your device token and subscription status are retained in our licensing database for as long as your subscription is active, so that Pro continues to work; cancelling your subscription lapses the entitlement.

Permissions

Guardrail requests only what it needs: storage (settings and the licence cache), alarms (periodic licence re-check), access to Salesforce domains (to provide its features on those pages), and access to our licensing endpoint. The cookies permission is optional and requested only if you turn on high-accuracy detection. All code runs locally in the extension; no remote code is downloaded or executed.

Your choices

You can clear all locally stored data by removing the extension, and revoke the optional cookie access by turning high-accuracy detection off. To request deletion of the device token and subscription record associated with your install, or with any question about this policy, contact us at cowijin@protonmail.com.
